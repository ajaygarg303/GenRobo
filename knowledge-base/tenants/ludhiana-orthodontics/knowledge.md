## Orthodontic treatments (braces & aligners)

### Options offered

- **Traditional metal braces**
- **Esthetic ceramic braces**
- **Lingual orthodontics** (invisible braces behind the teeth)
- **Customised lingual brackets and wires (i-Braces)**
- **Invisalign®** and **clear aligner** technologies
- **CLEAR ORTHO (Clear Aligners)** — transparent trays; suitable cases may not need fixed braces throughout treatment
- **Orthodontic microimplants**
- **Indirect bonding**

### Conditions commonly treated

- Protruded / forwardly placed teeth
- Spaces between teeth
- Anterior and posterior crossbite
- Deep bite, open bite (including habit-related and skeletal)
- Highly placed teeth
- Class II and Class III malocclusion
- Unerupted permanent teeth
- Unfavourable eruption
- Cleft lip and palate
- Facial asymmetry

### Types of appliances

**Removable appliances** — plastic and wire; patient removes for cleaning; supporting role, retention, growing children.

**Fixed appliances** — bonded for the treatment period; types include Begg (light wire), edgewise, and pre-adjusted edgewise (straight wire). Options include conventional brackets, **self-ligating** (e.g. SPEED, Damon), and **gold-plated stainless steel** (esthetics; suitable for nickel allergy).

### Invisalign / CLEAR ORTHO clear aligners

- Custom clear plastic aligners; nearly invisible
- Removable for eating, brushing, flossing
- Aligners changed about every **two weeks**; teeth move gradually
- Regular progress checks with the dentist/orthodontist
- Typical timeline often **6 months to 1 year** (case-dependent)
- Suitable for many cases including crowding, spacing, overbite, underbite, crossbite
- Tagline on site: *“We are decided to improve your smile with clear ortho — getting started couldn’t be simpler.”*

### Best time for orthodontic treatment (stages)

| Stage | Ages | Focus |
|-------|------|--------|
| **Stage I – Early** | 2–6 years | Underdeveloped arches, premature loss of primary teeth, thumb/finger habits; can often reduce later treatment needs |
| **Stage II – Mixed dentition** | 6–12 years | Permanent incisors and 6-year molars erupting; jaw relationships and alignment; highly responsive to forces |
| **Stage III – Adolescent** | Permanent dentition | Final bite relationship |

**Early treatment facts (from clinic):**

- ~75% of 12-year-olds may need orthodontic treatment, yet much of facial development is already complete by then
- Guiding facial development earlier (functional appliances) may correct ~80% of issues before adult teeth are fully in
- Children **8–11** are often more cooperative than **12–14**
- Phase One treatment can mean **less time** in fixed braces later
- ~55% of bad-bite cases involve under/overdeveloped jaws; functional appliances or limited braces may improve profile and bite in **7–9 months** in suitable cases

**All age groups** are welcome; adult orthodontic techniques are available for smile design.

## Advanced dental services (full clinic)

The clinic describes **“A to Z dental treatment”** and the following specialties:

### Orthodontics (Braces)

Malocclusions from tooth irregularity, jaw discrepancy, or both; dental displacement and facial growth modification where appropriate.

### Prosthodontics (Crowns / Bridges / Dentures)

Rehabilitation of missing or deficient teeth and tissues using biocompatible substitutes — complete/partial dentures, fixed bridges, crowns, implant-supported dentures, TMJ-related care, etc.

### Endodontics (Root Canal)

Tooth pulp and tissues around the root — root canal treatment, cracked teeth, dental trauma.

**Specialty highlight:** **Single sitting root canal treatment**

### Implantology (Dental Implants)

Artificial tooth roots supporting crowns/bridges; surgery under local or general anesthesia as needed; integration over **3–6 months** before final prosthesis.

**Specialty highlight:** **Immediate implants**

### Periodontics & children’s dentistry

Supporting structures of teeth and gum disease; care for children from birth through adolescence including sedation when needed.

### Cosmetic dentistry

Teeth whitening, gum bleaching, smile redesign, tooth jewellery, esthetic limited orthodontics, tooth-coloured fillings, composites, **CAD/CAM ceramic veneers**, all-ceramic crowns, gum recontouring for gummy smiles, **laser tooth whitening (Zoom advanced)**, smile makeover plans.

**Specialty highlights:** **Metal-free crowns**, **instant tooth whitening**, **esthetic tooth laminates**, **smile designing**

### Periodontics (gum disease)

Infection in gums and bone; bone grafting, flap surgery, repositioning soft tissue flaps, etc.

### Oral and maxillofacial surgery

Hard and soft tissues of head, mouth, jaws, neck — e.g. wisdom teeth removal, facial trauma, corrective jaw surgery.
