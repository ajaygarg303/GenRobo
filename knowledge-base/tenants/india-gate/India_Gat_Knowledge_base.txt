## Business overview

India Gate is a modern Indian takeaway in Tallaght, Dublin, established in 2010. The restaurant describes itself as a high-end takeaway offering authentic Indian cuisine: curries, biryanis, tandoor dishes, and traditional recipes with a contemporary touch. Chefs are trained in traditional Indian cooking.

## Location

1 Colberts Fort, Belgard Road, Tallaght, Dublin 24, Ireland.

Car parking is available on site.

## Opening hours

## Menu — starters (descriptions from website)

- **Aloo Tikki** — Cumin, ginger and chilli flavoured crunchy potato patties with a mint dip.
- **Vegetable Samosa** — Home-made crunchy short-dough pyramid turnovers with potato and mustard seed filling (described as 9 pieces on the site).
- **Onion Bhaji** — Finely sliced onion in spicy gram flour batter with coriander and fennel, cooked in ghee.
- **Paneer Pakoras** — Homemade cheese in gram flour batter with spices, deep fried.
- **Tandoori Mixed Grill** — Assortment from the clay oven: seekh kebab, chicken tikka and tandoori chicken; suitable for sharing.

## Menu — mains (descriptions from website)

- **Seekh Kebab** — Spring lamb mince with onion, ginger, garlic, coriander, mace and cardamom; skewered and barbecued in a clay oven; served with mint chutney.
- **Chicken Tikka Shashlik** — Chicken tikka with bell peppers, onions and scallions in a tangy sweet tomato, soy and garlic sauce.
- **Tikka Masala** — Mild and creamy; tomato and buttercream style sauce with cashew nuts.
- **Rogan Josh** — Rich brown sauce with caramelised onion, ginger, garlic, tomato, cinnamon and spices.
- **Jalfrezi** — Mixed peppers, tomatoes, onions, chillies and aromatic spices.

The website also mentions classics such as butter chicken in its philosophy text. For the full menu and current prices, direct customers to order online or call the restaurant.
